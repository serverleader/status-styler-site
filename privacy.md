# Status Styler Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-13

Status Styler does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- All text styling happens entirely on your device.
- The app has no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.
- The keyboard extension never requests Full Access, has no network
  capability, and cannot see or log what you type in other apps beyond
  inserting the characters you tap.
- The only data the app stores is your purchase state, kept locally on
  your device and in your iCloud/App Store account via Apple's standard
  in-app purchase system. Purchases are processed by Apple; we never see
  payment details.
